Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

1. Overview

Comic Bonk ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our decentralized application ("dApp") and mobile application.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or process any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or IP address on our servers.

Wallet Address: We read your public Solana wallet address only to:

  • Verify ownership of in-game assets (NFTs).
  • Display your public address on the leaderboard.
  • Facilitate transactions you initiate (e.g., minting).

We do NOT have access to your private keys, seed phrases, or funds. You retain full control over your wallet at all times.

3. Third-Party Infrastructure

Our application interacts with the Solana blockchain. To do this, we use third-party Remote Procedure Call (RPC) providers, including Helius.

When you interact with the blockchain, your public transaction data is broadcast to the Solana network and may be visible on public block explorers. RPC providers may log technical data (like IP addresses) for operational purposes, governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. NFT Minting & Transactions

All blockchain transactions are initiated and approved by you. We do not control the Solana network and cannot reverse transactions.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
